Artist and Interior Architect

(she/hun b.1992 Sydney) I am an interior architect and artist interested in the relationship between bodies and space. My practice spans curation, publishing, writing, and design. Through my work I am committed to challenging the inequalities that exist in space and spatial practices. My current areas of research include situated writing methods, queer and feminist spatial practices, the relationship between emotion, identity and space, and rethinking architecture through speculative fiction. In 2022 I co-founded the project space and platform NOGOODS and the magazine bias: bodies in architecture and structures with Danja Burchard, now co-run with Francesca Scapinello. My most recent curatorial and design projects include Hosting Space (June 2023) at Hordaland Kunstsenter in Bergen and Dissident Publics (May-June 2023) with NOGOODS and Exutoire at ROM for kunst og arkitektur in Oslo. I am currently based in Bergen and enjoy swimming, feminist science fiction and making furniture.
