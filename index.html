Artist and Interior Architect

(she/her b.1992 Sydney) I am an artist and interior architect educated in The Netherlands and Australia, now living in Bergen, Norway. I'm interested in space - the influence of architecture on individuals and society and its relationship to gender and sexuality. I work mostly with writing, performance and installation. I use architectural tools and methods in this work, questioning how history is embedded in our various architectures and what power dynamics are at play. I often focus on specific interior elements, such as furniture and textiles, and reflect on their philosophical and practical meanings as well as processes of production. I am co-founder of the studio and project space NOGOODS in Bergen. I enjoy swimming, feminist science fiction and making furniture.
