Maike Statz is an interior architect, artist and writer living in Oslo and Amsterdam. In her work she explores the relationship between bodies, emotion, behaviour, identity and space. Informed by feminist and queer practices, Maike investigates spatial inequalities and seeks alternative tools and methods for space-making.
